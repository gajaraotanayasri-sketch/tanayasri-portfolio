💻 My Projects

Warps and Wefts

Warps and Wefts is an innovative digital platform created to empower traditional weavers by giving them visibility, reach, and control over their craft. Artisans can seamlessly upload product information, showcase creations through high-quality visuals, and manage orders and customer interactions efficiently. The platform bridges local craftsmanship with global markets, helping even digitally inexperienced weavers expand opportunities and grow sustainably while preserving cultural heritage.

🦯

InvisionX

InvisionX is an advanced assistive navigation device for visually-impaired individuals. Integrating obstacle detection, directional feedback, and real-time spatial awareness, it allows users to move confidently in familiar and unfamiliar spaces. The intuitive interface gives clear, gentle cues while prioritizing simplicity, comfort, and independence. InvisionX reduces reliance on external help, promotes mobility, and enhances overall quality of life with empathetic, user-focused technology.

Rollex

Rollex is a smart painting device designed to improve precision, efficiency, and comfort for artists of all levels. Its ergonomic design reduces strain, allowing longer, focused sessions while ensuring smoother strokes and cleaner finishes. Rollex bridges imagination and execution, empowering artists to translate creative ideas into high-quality artwork consistently, with innovative functionality and intuitive operation that enhances the joy of painting.

Poigen

Poigen is a humane, practical pigeon repellent spray that gently discourages birds from lingering on balconies, ledges, and urban spaces. Safe for birds but effective in maintaining cleaner environments, it balances ethical considerations, usability, and functionality. Poigen demonstrates creating real-world, user-friendly solutions that respect living creatures while solving everyday problems, reflecting ethical and thoughtful design.

🕊️

Garuda

Garuda is a sound-engineered pigeon deterrent for large urban spaces. Using non-intrusive technology, it prevents birds from nesting or entering buildings, offering a sustainable long-term solution. Through Garuda, I explored strategic product design, innovation, and preventive approaches. The project demonstrates how technology can create practical, sustainable, and humane urban solutions for cleaner and safer environments.

Sonyc

Sonyc is a web-based music streaming and recommendation platform offering personalized, inclusive listening experiences. By analyzing metadata like genre, singer, and user preferences, it curates playlists tailored to individual tastes. With multi-language support and a smooth interface, Sonyc encourages exploration of new genres, artists, and compositions, creating a diverse, engaging hub for music lovers globally.

Personal Project

CropAlert

CropAlert is an AI-powered plant disease detection platform for rice farmers, designed to provide early, accurate, and actionable insights. Using image-based machine learning, it identifies major rice diseases, explains causes, visible symptoms, environmental risk factors, and affordable management strategies. CropAlert bridges agricultural science and technology, helping farmers prevent crop losses and implement sustainable farming practices, making a tangible real-world impact.
